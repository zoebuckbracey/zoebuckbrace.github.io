About Me

As a Senior Science Educator and Director of Learning for a Just and Sustainable Future at BSCS Science Learning, I work in collaboration with educators, administrators, students, and scholars to:

  • lead cross-functional teams in the design of comprehensive resources to support teaching and learning
  • design and deliver engaging, scalable, and transformative professional learning programs for educators, facilitators, and leaders
  • conduct data-driven research and evaluation of program effectiveness to provides meaningful insights into user experience

I am co-founder of the BSCS Equity and Social Justice Initiative, and my work is focused on science instruction that centers the voices of Black, Indigenous, and Latine students and educators, as well as students and educators across the spectra of gender and sexuality, particularly in physics, astronomy, and Earth science.

I live in Colorado Springs, CO, a state made up of lands tied to 48 contemporary tribal nations.
